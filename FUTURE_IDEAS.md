# Future Ideas

## Expert Lens — "See through their eyes"
Paste a URL (competitor landing page, pricing page, product page) and pick an expert. The page renders with their annotations overlaid — specific, actionable feedback on real product surfaces. Highly shareable.

## Roast My Pitch
Write a 2-paragraph product idea and experts stress-test it. Where's the moat? Who's the real competitor? What assumption are you making that's probably wrong? Useful for earliest-stage founders.

## Strategy Pressure Test
Describe a specific decision you're about to make ("switching from sales-led to PLG") and experts with relevant experience tell you what they've seen go wrong, what to watch for, what metrics to track. Grounded in their actual experiences at real companies.

## Product Autopsy
Input a real product or company. The system assembles a panel of experts who each dissect it from their domain — growth, metrics, strategy, retention. Output is a structured teardown document.

## Founder Simulation — "Your advisory board"
Describe your startup once — stage, market, team, challenge. The system builds a persistent advisory board of 3-4 experts matched to your situation. They remember context across sessions. Turns a tool into a relationship.
